Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

UnLink: No Contact Tracker is designed to help you manage no-contact recovery privately. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

The short version: UnLink does not require an account, does not run ads, does not use analytics, and does not send your recovery data to our servers.

Information You Enter

You may choose to enter information such as names, relationship details, notes, mood entries, urge logs, recovery goals, money-saved inputs, and time-reclaimed inputs. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage.

Local Backups and Exports

UnLink can create local backups on your device. If you choose to export or share a backup, you control where it is sent, such as Files, AirDrop, or another service you select. UnLink does not upload those exports to a server operated by us.

Subscriptions and Payments

Optional subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Apple may process information according to its own privacy policy.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account credentials
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics data
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or messages from your device
  • No recovery notes or journal entries sent to our servers

Third-Party Services

UnLink does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. The app uses Apple’s App Store services for subscription purchases and restores. Apple’s handling of purchase-related information is governed by Apple’s policies.

Contacting Us

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you include in your message. We use it only to respond to your request or provide support.

Children's Privacy

UnLink is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support email, please contact us and we will address the request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when changes are published.
