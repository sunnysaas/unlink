Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the App Store app, tap your profile picture, then tap Subscriptions. Select UnLink and choose Cancel Subscription. Apple manages App Store billing and cancellations.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through Apple’s “Report a Problem” page or from your App Store purchase history.

Where is my no contact data stored?

UnLink is designed as a privacy-first app. Your no contact progress, reflections, reminders, and recovery-related entries are intended to stay on your device unless the app clearly states otherwise.

Why are notifications not working?

Check that notifications are enabled for UnLink in iOS Settings. Open Settings, tap Notifications, choose UnLink, and make sure notifications are allowed.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Choose “Feature request” in the support form and describe what would make UnLink more useful for your no contact journey or breakup recovery.

Is this app a replacement for professional help?

No. UnLink is a self-tracking and reflection tool. It is not medical, mental health, legal, or crisis advice. If you feel unsafe or at risk, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional.
